  • How Does Students’ Prior Knowledge Affect Their Learning?

    Principle: Students ’ prior knowledge can help or hinder learning

    • WHAT DOES THE RESEARCH TELL US ABOUT PRIOR KNOWLEDGE?

      Numerous studies have demonstrated that connecting new knowledge to old increases the recall and retention of that information. However, creating these connections may not occur naturally, and as educators, it's our responsibility to assist students in capitalizing on their knowledge and experiences. The lack of connection between topics is particularly noticeable when transferring knowledge between different fields of study. For example, students may struggle to apply their knowledge of physics and/or chemistry to comprehend biological concepts.
